# Microdocx Brand Voice & Identity

## Brand Promise
"Free, offline games with smart AI opponents — no signup, no ads required"

## Brand Personality
- Modern & playful
- Inclusive & accessible
- Friendly & approachable
- Tech-savvy but not intimidating

## Tone of Voice
- Enthusiastic without being over-the-top
- Clear and jargon-free
- Encouraging and supportive
- Respectful of player time and privacy

## Tagline Options

1. "Play Anywhere. Solo or Social. Always Free AI."
   - Focus: Flexibility and value

2. "Games That Play With You—Not Against You"
   - Focus: AI friendliness and accessibility

3. "Your Favorite Board Games. Online. With Smart AI."
   - Focus: Familiarity and AI differentiation

4. "No Friends Needed. Free AI Opponent Included."
   - Focus: Solo play and inclusivity

5. "Board Games for Today. Always Free. Always Offline."
   - Focus: Modern convenience and reliability

## Primary Tagline (Selected)
"Games That Play With You—Not Against You"

## Visual Identity
- Logo: Modern gamepad icon with "MICRODOCX" text
- Primary Color: #3EDD03 (vibrant green)
- Secondary Color: #808488 (professional gray)
- Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif
- Style: Minimal, approachable, gaming-forward

## Brand Values
✓ Free always (Easy AI is never behind a paywall)
✓ Offline first (no internet required to play)
✓ Privacy-conscious (no accounts, minimal data collection)
✓ Inclusive (works across age groups and skill levels)
✓ Quality-focused (professional presentation, good game design)
