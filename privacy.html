Privacy Policy — Microdocx Games

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Microdocx ("we", "us", "our") operates the Microdocx game studio website and mobile applications (collectively, the "Services"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Player Name: Optional display name for leaderboards and achievements. Stored locally on your device only.
  • Payment Information: For in-app purchases, payment processing is handled by third-party providers (Apple, Google, Razorpay). We do not store credit card data.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Game Data: Leaderboard scores, achievements, game statistics stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly sync it.
  • Analytics: Anonymous game play events (game started, game completed, AI difficulty selected) sent to Google Analytics 4. This data is anonymized and cannot identify you personally.
  • Ads: When you view ads, Google AdSense and Google AdMob collect information to serve relevant advertisements. See their privacy policies for details.
  • Device Information: We may collect device model, OS version, app version, and crash logs to improve stability and performance.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • We do NOT collect your real name, email, phone number, or location without explicit permission.
  • We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do NOT create user accounts or require login.
  • We do NOT store game data on our servers — all data is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To Improve Services: Game analytics help us understand which games are popular and how to improve gameplay.
  • To Display Ads: Analytics and ad data help show you relevant advertisements.
  • To Debug Issues: Crash reports and error logs help us fix bugs.
  • To Show Leaderboards: Your player name and score are shown on leaderboards only on your device. They are not shared globally unless you explicitly choose to share.

5. Data Storage & Security

5.1 Local Storage

All game data (leaderboards, achievements, statistics, streaks) is stored locally on your device using browser storage (localStorage). This data:

  • Never leaves your device automatically
  • Is deleted if you clear browser/app data
  • Is specific to your device — different devices have different leaderboards

5.2 Analytics Data

Anonymous analytics events are sent to Google Analytics 4 servers. Google's infrastructure uses industry-standard security measures.

5.3 No Database

We do not maintain a database of user information. Microdocx games are stateless — they do not require accounts or cloud storage.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Some Microdocx games (Tiny Ludo, Times Table Arena, Pixel Quest) are designed for children under 13.

  • We do NOT collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone) from children.
  • Player names are optional and stored only on the child's device.
  • Parents can clear game data anytime via device settings.
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

7. Your Rights & Choices

7.1 Access Your Data

All your game data is on your device. You can view it anytime by opening the game's Statistics or Leaderboard screen.

7.2 Delete Your Data

  • Clear Browser/App Data: Go to your device settings and clear app cache/data to delete all local game data.
  • Reset Leaderboard: Most games have a "Reset Statistics" button in Settings.

7.3 Opt Out of Analytics

You can disable Google Analytics tracking:

  • In most games, go to Settings → turn off "Analytics"
  • Install Google Analytics Opt-out browser extension: https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

7.4 Disable Ads

You can purchase ad-free versions of games through in-app purchases.

8. GDPR & International Privacy Laws

8.1 European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union:

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Since we store data only on your device, you have full control
  • You can delete all data anytime by clearing app cache
  • We do not transfer data outside the EU

8.2 India Users (DISHA/India Privacy Laws)

Microdocx is operated from India and complies with India's data protection principles.
